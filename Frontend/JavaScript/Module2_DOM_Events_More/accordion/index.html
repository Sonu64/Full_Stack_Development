FAQ

How do I set up my budgets and what happens if I overspend a category?

▶

Setting up your budget is easy! Navigate to the "Budget" tab and use the plus button to create a new category (e.g., Groceries, Entertainment, Bills). You assign a monetary limit and a time period (usually monthly). If you log an expense that exceeds the allocated budget for that category, the app will instantly send you a notification alert and the budget bar for that category will visually change (e.g., turn red) on your dashboard. This provides real-time feedback so you can adjust your spending immediately.

Does the app automatically track my spending, or do I have to enter every expense manually?

▶

You must enter your expenses manually. The app is designed to give you complete control and awareness of every transaction by requiring manual input. You can quickly add expenses on the go by selecting the category and entering the amount. This hands-on method helps reinforce good spending habits and ensures you precisely categorize all purchases, including those made with cash.
